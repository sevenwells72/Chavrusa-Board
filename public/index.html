Find a Chavrusa

Post what you want to learn. Browse open requests. Connect through private relay - no profiles, no pressure.

Chavrusashaft is a simple bulletin board for finding a learning partner. Here's how it works: you post a request with what you want to learn, your availability, and your preferred style. Others browse the board and reach out privately if it's a good fit. There are no profiles to set up, no matching algorithms, and no one sees your info until you're ready to connect. Think of it like a shul bulletin board - but online, and open to everyone.
